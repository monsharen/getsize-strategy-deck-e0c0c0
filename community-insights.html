The Adizero Evo SL runs slightly small. People who own an Asics running shoe are typically recommended about half a size larger in the Evo SL, so plan to size up. Use the tool above to get your exact size from a shoe you already own.

The Adizero Evo SL runs about half a size smaller than Asics. If you wear EU 44 in Asics, you are likely EU 44⅔ to 45 in the Evo SL, based on people in our community who own both.

Adidas is a German sportswear giant founded in 1949. It is one of the largest athletic shoe manufacturers worldwide, known for its three-stripe trademark and technologies like Boost, 4DFWD and Lightstrike cushioning.

Adidas sizing varies by category. Running shoes typically run true to size; lifestyle models fit true but can feel narrow; football boots are snug by design. For most Adidas sneakers, order your regular size.
